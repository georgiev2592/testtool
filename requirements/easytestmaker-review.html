1.5.3. EasyTestMaker

EasyTestMaker is an online tool for generating an taking tests. The site offers three tiers of payment plans yearly with more features depending on the amount paid. The first plan is free, the next is $49.99/year (Plus), and the last is $74.99/year (Premium). The different plans seem to offer adequate to exceptional features.

Good Features:

  • Some automatic formatting and spell check
  • Exam builder and automatically generated alternate versions
  • Export to PDF, Word, and Excel
  • Upload images, charts or graphs
  • Publish any test to be taken online.
  • Automatic grading
  • Time limits
Bad Features:
  • Reviews comment on a buggy interface
  • Most good features require payment and could not be confirmed
Missing Features:
  • No difficulty ratings
